About me

I am now an assitant professor in Department of Computer Science and Statistics at University of Rhode Island. I obtained a PhD in Statistics at University of Florida in Aug. 2016. My research area concerns about dimension reduction and variable selection of high dimensional data. I hope to extend my research repertoire and look forward to collaborate with many researchers from different fields such as machine learning, genetics, and epidemiology.
